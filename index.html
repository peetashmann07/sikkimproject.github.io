About Sikkim

Sikkim is a small state located in the North-East of India. Sikkim is the least populated and second smallest state of India. It borders, Tibet, Bhutan and Nepal. Sikkim is also known for its biodiversity and its subtropical climate. India's highest mountain peak, Kangchenjunga resides in Sikkim. The capital city of Sikkim is Gangtok. The Khangchendzonga National Park of Sikkim has been awarded with UNESCO World Heritage Site.
